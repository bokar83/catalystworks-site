What lands on your desk

Not a deck. Not a discovery phase. One written map you can act on Monday, and a live walkthrough so nothing gets lost in translation.

  • A written map of your business, bottleneck by bottleneckPlain language, no jargon, no filler. What is actually in the way, laid out so you can see it.
  • Every bottleneck ranked by what it costs youIn dollars and hours a month, so the order to fix them is obvious, not a matter of opinion.
  • The one fix to make first, named and scopedSpecific enough that you or your team can start on it the next morning.
  • A 30-minute walkthrough of the map, liveI take you through it, you ask the hard questions, we make sure it is right before I leave.

What the five days look like

Fixed scope, fixed clock. You know exactly what happens and when.

  • Day 1
    I learn your business
    A working session with you and the people closest to the work. I listen for where things stall, not where the org chart says they should.
  • Days 2 to 3
    I trace where it actually goes
    I follow the time, the money, and the decisions through your business. The friction shows me the constraint. The org chart rarely does.
  • Day 4
    I rank the bottlenecks
    Every one, ordered by what it costs you. Then I name the single one to fix first, and I scope it so it is ready to hand to whoever does the work.
  • Day 5
    You get the map
    The written map lands, and I walk you through it live for 30 minutes. You leave knowing exactly what to fix, why it is first, and what it is worth.
