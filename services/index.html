How I work

You run the business.
I run the systems with you.

Most consultants hand you a deck and a bill. I find the one thing slowing you down, fix it with the right tools, and stay long enough that your people can run it without me.

The order matters

Every business has more than one problem. Almost none of them are the one worth fixing first. The job is to find the constraint, fix that, and leave your team able to keep it fixed. In that order.

  • Find the constraint.Not a list of everything wrong. The single thing that, once fixed, moves the number. Most of what looks urgent is noise sitting on top of it.
  • Fix what matters most first.The right tools for your business, wired and working. AI when it earns its place on the problem, not because it is the thing everyone is talking about this quarter.
  • Stay and run it with you.I train your people on what ships, hands on the keyboard, so the fix holds after I am gone. Not a handshake and a PDF. A system your team owns.

Three ways in

Start with the audit. Most people do. If you already know the fix and just need it built, we skip to the sprint. If you want an operator who stays, we do that too.
