01Why this room

Two questions every owner is quietly asking

"What is actually worth using. And how do I avoid getting burned trying."

Those are the two real questions. Not "what is a large language model." The owners I talk to are past that. They want to know where AI saves them real hours, where it quietly costs them, and what a person running a small business should do on Monday morning.

The honest answer does not come from a vendor with a demo to sell. It comes from someone who runs his own business on the AI he is talking about, and who spent a decade in HR at a Fortune 100 where helping people actually adopt new tools, in person and over video, was part of the work.

That is the room this workshop builds. Plain English. No hype. A live system you can watch work, and a thing you build yourself before you leave.
