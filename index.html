    Constraint statement

    "Your throughput is bounded by an information bottleneck at the operations-to-finance handoff. Capacity, capital, and demand are not the issue."

    Three friction signals

    1. Operations has visibility into job-level margin in real time. Finance does not see margin until month-close. Decisions get made on stale numbers.
    2. Three top performers in the last 18 months left for "more strategic" roles. The strategic work exists. It is buried under reporting overhead.
    3. The CRM and the accounting system disagree on three of the top ten clients. Nobody owns reconciliation. Each function trusts its own number.

    One concrete action before Friday

    Schedule a 45-minute walkthrough between operations lead and finance lead, with one shared spreadsheet listing every client and the gross margin each function believes is true. Reconcile in the room.
