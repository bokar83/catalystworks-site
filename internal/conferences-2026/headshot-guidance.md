# Headshot Specs + Production Guidance

What every conference will require, and two paths to producing it.

---

## Technical specs (all 3 conferences accept this)

| Spec | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Resolution | 1200 x 1200 px minimum, square crop |
| Format | JPEG (RGB, sRGB color space) |
| File size | Under 500 KB after optimization |
| Filename | `boubacar-barry-headshot-2026.jpg` |
| Background | Neutral light gray (#E5E7EB) or off-white (#F8F8F8) |
| Framing | Shoulders-up. Eyes on the upper third of the frame |
| Eye contact | Direct to camera lens |
| Lighting | Soft and even. No hard shadows under chin or nose |
| Expression | Warm smile, lips closed or slightly parted. Not toothy |
| Wardrobe | Collared shirt OR merino crew sweater. Solid color. No graphic tee. No suit jacket unless the role calls for it |
| Color guidance | Navy, charcoal, forest green, or warm white work well on the neutral bg |
| Post-processing | Color-correct, soft skin retouch only. NO heavy filter, NO blur background, NO Lensa-style AI |

---

## Path A: self-shoot (free, 30 minutes)

Works fine for AI Tinkerers SLC. May work for MicroConf and AfroTech if executed cleanly. Try this first.

**Setup:**

1. Pick a north-facing window between 10:00 and 14:00 (soft indirect daylight)
2. Stand 5 to 6 feet back from the window so light hits your face at a 45 degree angle
3. Hang a neutral light gray sheet, foam board, or seamless paper 4 feet behind you
4. Phone on a tripod at eye level, 5 feet in front of you
5. Pair AirPods to your phone and use the volume button on the AirPods stem to fire the shutter without touching the phone

**Capture:**

- Take 30 to 50 frames across 3 to 4 expressions (slight smile, warmer smile, neutral, mid-laugh)
- Switch between 3 micro-poses (square to camera, slight left shoulder forward, slight right)
- Review every 10 frames so you can correct lighting drift

**Edit:**

- Crop to 1:1 square, shoulders to crown of head
- Lift exposure slightly if shadow side of face is muddy
- Reduce highlights on forehead if window light blew them out
- Export at 1200 x 1200, JPEG, quality 85, sRGB

**Tools that work:**

- Phone: Lightroom Mobile (free tier sufficient)
- Desktop: Affinity Photo, GIMP, or Photoshop

## Path B: pro shoot (paid, 1 to 2 hours)

Worth it if AfroTech accepts the keynote slot. The visibility delta on the speaker grid justifies the spend.

**Budget guidance for SLC market:**

- Entry tier: $150 to $250 for a 30 to 45 minute session, 5 to 10 retouched files
- Mid tier: $300 to $500 for a 1 hour session, 10 to 20 retouched files, multiple wardrobe changes
- Premium tier: $600 to $1200 for executive headshot specialists with studio + assistant

See `headshot-options.md` for 3 specific SLC photographer suggestions to contact.

**What to ask the photographer:**

1. Do you shoot against seamless light gray or off-white? (If they only do dark moody backgrounds, skip them for this purpose.)
2. Can you deliver square crops at 1200 x 1200 minimum?
3. How many retouched files included in the base price?
4. Turnaround time for the final files?
5. Are RAW files released if I want to do additional crops later?

**On-the-day prep:**

- Bring 2 wardrobe options (one with collar, one without)
- Bring the exact shirt you plan to ship; do not arrive in a t-shirt and change later (collar creases)
- Schedule for the morning. Late-afternoon faces sag.
- Eat 90 minutes before, no caffeine within 60 minutes (jitter shows in micro-expressions)

## Delivery prep

Once you have the final file, create 3 versions for portability:

| Version | Use case |
|---|---|
| `boubacar-headshot-1200.jpg` | Conference speaker grids, LinkedIn profile |
| `boubacar-headshot-2400.jpg` | Print programs, large speaker walls |
| `boubacar-headshot-square-light.jpg` | Light-mode bg, default |
| `boubacar-headshot-square-dark.jpg` | Dark-mode bg variant for venues that go dark |

Upload all 4 to Google Drive in a folder named `2026-speaker-kit/headshots/` and share view-only link in the CFP submission notes field.

---

## What NOT to use

- Existing LinkedIn profile photo if it is more than 18 months old
- Any photo with another person cropped out (visible edge artifacts)
- Selfies from any social platform (compression artifacts, wrong aspect)
- Cropped wedding or family photos
- AI-generated headshots (Lensa, Aragon, ProfilePicture.ai). Conferences detect these and reject.
