# CFP Submission Checklist

Step-by-step per conference. Estimated time per step. Submit all 3 in a 3.5-hour block.

---

## Pre-flight (do once, applies to all 3)

| # | Step | Time |
|---|---|---|
| P1 | Read `bios.md`, copy the 100-word and 250-word variants to clipboard manager | 5 min |
| P2 | Read `talk-abstracts.md`, confirm each conference is matched to the right abstract | 5 min |
| P3 | Capture or finalize headshot per `headshot-guidance.md`. Drop into Google Drive `2026-speaker-kit/headshots/` | 30 to 180 min |
| P4 | Record + upload sample reel per `sample-reel-script.md`. YouTube unlisted. Copy URL | 180 min (skip if already have one) |
| P5 | Confirm LinkedIn profile is current (headline matches positioning, latest role shows Catalyst Works) | 10 min |
| P6 | Confirm catalystworks.consulting loads and the contact form works | 5 min |

**Pre-flight total: 55 min (if headshot + reel already exist) or ~6 hours (if both need fresh production).**

Once pre-flight is done, the 3 CFP submissions below are quick.

## 1. AI Tinkerers Salt Lake City

**URL:** https://slc.aitinkerers.org/

**Format:** Local meetup, rolling submissions. Demo format (20 minutes + Q&A).

**Assigned abstract:** "The Operator Stack: How I Run 5 Brands Solo With Claude, n8n, and a $200 VPS"

**Bio to paste:** 100-word variant from `bios.md`.

| # | Step | Time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Open https://slc.aitinkerers.org/ in browser | 1 min |
| 2 | Find the "Propose a Demo" or "Speak" link (typically in header or footer). If only a Luma group exists, join the Luma and DM the SLC organizer with the abstract | 5 min |
| 3 | Paste talk title + 100-word bio + abstract from `talk-abstracts.md` Section 1 | 10 min |
| 4 | Paste sample reel URL in "Have you presented before?" field. Link to one prior agentsHQ technical writeup or demo if available | 5 min |

**Step total: ~20 min.**

**Notes:**

- AI Tinkerers is community-run. Expect the SLC organizer to reply directly (not a formal program committee).
- Demo format means: bring laptop, bring stack running, expect informal Q&A. No slide deck required (slides actively discouraged in most chapters).
- If the next event is full, ask to be slotted into the following month.

## 3. AfroTech Conference 2026 (Houston, Nov 2-6)

**URL:** https://app.sessionboard.com/submit/afrotech-conference-2026/36e83672-778f-402c-90b7-eed6a5592466

**Format:** Multi-track. Keynote, breakout (30 min), or workshop (60 to 90 min). 25,000+ attendees, largest Black tech conference in the US.

**Assigned abstract:** "First-Gen Operator: Building an AI Agency From Salt Lake City Utah With Guinean Roots"

**Bio to paste:** 250-word variant from `bios.md`.

| # | Step | Time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Open submission URL. Create SessionBoard account if first time (Sign Up with Google → use boubacar@catalystworks.consulting) | 8 min |
| 2 | Complete speaker profile: full name, pronouns (he/him), location, time zone (America/Denver), preferred contact email | 5 min |
| 3 | Upload headshot from Drive. SessionBoard requires direct upload (not link) | 3 min |
| 4 | Add social links: LinkedIn, X, catalystworks.consulting, humanatwork.ai | 5 min |
| 5 | Select session type: Breakout (30 min) is the right call for a first-time speaker. Keynote later. | 2 min |
| 6 | Paste talk title + abstract from `talk-abstracts.md` Section 3 | 5 min |
| 7 | Paste 250-word bio. Fill "speaker statement" field with: "First-generation Guinean operator. First time at AfroTech. The talk shares the architecture and the cost numbers behind a solo AI consultancy without the typical founder polish." | 8 min |
| 8 | Paste sample reel YouTube URL in "speaker video" field. Confirm submission. Save the confirmation email | 4 min |

**Step total: ~40 min.**

**Notes:**

- AfroTech has the deepest applicant pool of the 3. The angle is the differentiator. Lean into first-gen + Guinean origin + technical depth. Do not soften it.
- Breakout slot is the right ask for a first-time. Workshop or keynote is too big a step.
- If accepted, AfroTech may request a 5 to 10 minute pre-recorded preview. Reuse the sample reel for this.
- Track applicants get notified 60 to 90 days before the event.
