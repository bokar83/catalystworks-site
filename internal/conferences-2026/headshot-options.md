# SLC Headshot Photographer Options

Three suggested contacts in the Salt Lake City area. Verify pricing and availability directly — the conference photography market shifts quarterly.

> **Note:** Live web research was rate-limited during this prep. Names below are based on the established SLC professional headshot market segment ($150 to $500 single-session range). Confirm each business is still operating and the price tier still matches before booking. Cross-reference with Google Maps + recent Google reviews + their current portfolio site.

---

## Option 1: Entry tier ($150 to $250)

**Type:** Mobile or studio. 30 to 45 minute session. 5 to 10 retouched files.

**Where to look:**

- Search Google Maps for "headshot photographer Salt Lake City" filtered to 4.8+ star reviews with 50+ reviews
- Filter for businesses that explicitly list "LinkedIn headshots" or "professional headshots" in their service menu (not wedding or family photographers who also do headshots)
- Recently active Instagram (posts within last 30 days) is a stronger signal of current operation than website alone

**Typical names in this tier:** independent photographers operating out of co-working studios, "headshot day" pop-ups, or newer studio brands competing on price.

**What to ask:**

- "Do you shoot against light gray or off-white seamless?"
- "Can you deliver square 1200 x 1200 minimum?"
- "How many wardrobe changes included?"

---

## Option 2: Mid tier ($300 to $500)

**Type:** Dedicated studio. 1 hour session. 10 to 20 retouched files. Multiple wardrobe changes. Same-week turnaround.

**Where to look:**

- Salt Lake City executive headshot specialists (search "executive headshot Salt Lake City")
- LinkedIn portfolio link is the strongest signal — check the photographer's own LinkedIn and look at clients they have tagged
- Studio brands with 3+ years of operation and a stable URL

**Typical names in this tier:** established studios with named lead photographers, often with downtown SLC or Sugar House addresses.

**What to ask:**

- "Do you have a corporate headshot package separate from your branding shoots?"
- "Do RAW files release for an additional fee?"
- "Can the session be scheduled for morning light?"

---

## Option 3: Premium tier ($600+)

**Type:** Full studio with assistant. 2 hour session. Wardrobe consultation. 20+ retouched files. Multiple background options. Lifetime usage rights.

**Where to look:**

- Photographers who specifically market to "executives, speakers, and thought leaders"
- Studios with a published rate card and visible client list including conference speakers or published authors
- Recommendations from local SLC business networks (Silicon Slopes, WTC Utah, Utah Black Chamber)

**When this tier is worth it:**

- AfroTech accepts you for a keynote slot
- You expect to use the same headshot for 3+ years across multiple high-visibility surfaces
- The photographer has a documented track record with first-time conference speakers and can coach posture + expression

## Booking script

Reusable email or DM template:

```
Subject: 1 hour headshot session for conference speaker kit

Hi [name],

I'm looking to book a 1-hour headshot session for an upcoming conference speaker kit (3 conferences applying to in the next month). Goal is a single hero shot I can use across LinkedIn, the conference speaker grids, and my website.

Requirements:
- Square crop, 1200x1200 minimum, JPEG
- Light gray or off-white seamless background
- Shoulders-up framing, direct eye contact
- 10+ retouched files delivered

What does pricing and next-available look like? Mornings preferred for the lighting.

Thanks,
Boubacar
catalystworks.consulting
```

---

## Verification protocol before booking

Before paying any deposit:

1. View 3+ recent samples that match the spec (light bg, square crop, professional dress, direct eye contact). If their portfolio is all moody-dark or all environmental-outdoor, they are not the right fit.
2. Confirm turnaround in writing. "1 week" is the target. "2 weeks" is acceptable. "3+ weeks" misses the MicroConf submission window.
3. Confirm what happens if you do not love the final files. Reshoot policy or partial refund.
4. Pay deposit only by credit card or Stripe Link (chargeback protection). Avoid Venmo or Zelle for first-time vendors.
